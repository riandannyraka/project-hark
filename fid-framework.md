## Usage Notes for AI Agents

- **To generate new character:** Fill pillars 1–5 in order. Summary last.
- **To analyze an existing character:** Extract from source text. Leave gaps as `[UNKNOWN]`.
- **To maintain consistency:** Before writing FID passage, review Pillars 2 (metaphors) and 4 (refuge).
- **To create conflict:** Push the character into a scene that blocks Pillar 4 (primary refuge) and requires Pillar 3 (forbidden word).
- **To evolve a character:** After a breakthrough event, modify Pillar 1 (syntax pattern under stress) and Pillar 2 (one metaphor may weaken or break).
