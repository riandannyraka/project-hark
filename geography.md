## HOW TO DRAW THIS FULL MAP

### Step 1 – Continent Outline
Draw an **irregular pentagon** stretched east-west, tapering at the south into a point.

### Step 2 – Realms (Color or Shade 5 Zones)
- **West:** Desert yellow/orange
- **North:** White/grey (mountains)
- **East:** Green (plains)
- **South:** Blue (islands)
- **Center:** Purple or special color (neutral)

### Step 3 – Add Capitals
- **West:** City symbol on Gilded Eye shore
- **North:** Fortress symbol near The Needle
- **East:** Estate symbols (no single large capital)
- **South:** Port symbol on largest island
- **Center:** Star/citadel symbol at exact middle

### Step 4 – Add Natural Landmarks
- Gilded Eye (large lake, west)
- The Needle (tall peak, north)
- Salt flats (west of Gilded Eye)
- Glaciers (north coast)
- Deep sea trenches (south, marked with ~ lines)
- Wide slow rivers (east, blue lines)
- Small mountain range (southern edge of center zone)

### Step 5 – Add Secrets (Optional – for GM/DM use)
- Mark "Hidden Heir" (west desert monastery)
- Mark "Hidden Bone-mages" (north crypts, three locations)
- Mark "Hidden Grandson" (east, wealthiest estate)
- Mark "Hidden Mages" (south, seven scattered dots)
- Mark "The Vault / Something Sleeping" (beneath The Cradle – draw as underground symbol)
