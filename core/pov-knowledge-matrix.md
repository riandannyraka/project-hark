# DEBT OF A KIN: POV KNOWLEDGE MATRIX

## Purpose
To prevent omniscient bleed – the accidental inclusion of information a character could not possess. Every scene must be filtered through what the POV character *actually* knows, suspects, or can observe. This matrix lists **Known** (facts they possess), **Suspected** (informed guesses), **Unaware** (critical blind spots), and **Misunderstands** (false beliefs).

---

## HARK (Damian Whiters)

### KNOWS (Explicit facts he has)

- **His own past:**
  - Birth name Damian Whiters, abandoned at age 7 to the Order.
  - Trained as a Fire Gate / Light mutation.
  - Lea (Wind Gate) was his first friend; she was killed by the Order during their escape attempt (age 10).
  - He killed the instructor and one handler during the escape.
  - Lived in the forest for five years (ages 10–15), nearly died multiple times.
  - Joel the mercenary saved him, named him “Hark,” taught him Edge and survival.
  - Joel died of blood cancer after less than a month.

- **His magical debt:**
  - Frost‑scars on hands and forearms from Light overdraw.
  - His internal reservoir is shallow; using magic would likely kill him.
  - He has not used magic since age 10.

- **His psychological FID rules:**
  - He consciously suppresses naming fear, isolation, desire.
  - He experiences physical stress (tight chest, faster pulse, dissociation) when approaching those emotions.
  - He knows the rules are self‑inflicted, not magical.

- **The job and Renne:**
  - He took a contract to find “the Seed” (Renne).
  - He knows Renne is a healer who runs a network for Sensitive children.
  - He knows he spent four months with a young woman (name: Renne) sixteen years ago, by a river, in a cottage.
  - He remembers her scarred hands, her voice, the dried flower he kept.
  - He knows they conceived a child together (but believes the child was stillborn or died – he never saw the baby).
  - **He does NOT know that the child survived or that Alexandria is his daughter.**

- **The Order hunt:**
  - He knows five Order teams are hunting Renne.
  - He has killed Team 5 (Ellera, Calen) and Team 4 (Vyktor, Rask) personally.
  - He knows Grey Kestrel (Team 2) is a Razor‑level Edge fighter.
  - He knows Orin is a Savant and Alexandria is a Silencer (but not their names or faces until late).

- **Current situation (end of Volume 1):**
  - He is comatose. He knows nothing after collapsing in the grotto.

### SUSPECTS (Informed guesses, not confirmed)

- That Renne might still care for him (based on her reaction at the reunion).
- That the Order will not stop hunting her even if he kills all five teams.
- That his FID rules are crumbling (he has moments of near‑breakthrough).

### UNAWARE (Critical blind spots)

- **About his daughter:**
  - He does NOT know Alexandria exists.
  - He does NOT know that the young Silencer who saved his life is his biological child.
  - He does NOT know that Renne gave the baby to the foundling house.

- **About Renne’s network:**
  - He does not know Marta, Elias (he met Elias but doesn’t know Elias works for Renne), Lina, or Jax beyond surface acquaintance.
  - He does not know the full extent of Renne’s operation.

- **About Orin’s knowledge:**
  - He does not know Orin knows the truth about Alexandria.

- **About his own future:**
  - He has no idea if he will wake from the coma.

### MISUNDERSTANDS (False beliefs)

- He believes the baby (Alexandria) died or was lost – because Renne never told him otherwise.
- He believes his FID rules make him safe; in truth, they make him brittle.
- He believes he does not “want” Renne – but his actions (keeping the flower, hunting her across a continent) contradict this.

## RENNE (The Seed)

### KNOWS (Explicit facts she has)

- **Her own past:**
  - Escaped the Order at age 15.
  - Has the Healing mutation (Water Gate).
  - Has been running the network for Sensitive children for ~16 years.
  - Surrendered her daughter Alexandria to the foundling house at birth.

- **About Hark:**
  - She knows Hark (Damian) was a Fire Gate / Light mage.
  - She knows his frost‑scars, his silence, his self‑imposed FID rules.
  - She knows he is the father of her daughter.
  - She knows he does NOT know about Alexandria (she never told him).
  - She knows he kept the dried flower for fifteen years.

- **About her network:**
  - She knows Marta, Elias, Lina, Jax (by the end).
  - She knows the locations of several safehouses (most now burned).
  - She knows the Order is using blood‑trail hounds and leashed mages.

- **About the Order teams:**
  - She knows Team 5 and Team 4 are dead (killed by Hark).
  - She knows Team 2 was killed in the grotto (Kestrel by Hark, Blood‑mage by the young Silencer).
  - She does not know the young Silencer is her daughter.

- **Current situation (end of Volume 1):**
  - Hark is comatose, she is caring for him in the windmill.
  - She has Lina, Marta, and Jax with her.
  - She knows Orin falsified the report (she doesn’t know his name, but she knows someone lied to save them).

### SUSPECTS

- That the young Silencer (Alexandria) might be connected to her – because of Lina’s drawing and the grey eyes.
- That Hark’s coma might break his FID rules if he wakes.
- That the Order will eventually find them again.

### UNAWARE

- **About her daughter:**
  - She does NOT know that the young Silencer (Alexandria) is her biological daughter.
  - She only suspects a vague resemblance.

- **About Orin:**
  - She does not know Orin’s name or that he falsified the report deliberately.

- **About Hark’s full history:**
  - She does not know about Lea, Joel, or the details of his escape from the Cradle.

### MISUNDERSTANDS

- She believes she is solely responsible for Alexandria’s fate – but the Order’s system shares blame.
- She believes she does not deserve happiness or love – a false belief that keeps her from reaching out to Hark more directly.

## ALEXANDRIA

### KNOWS (Explicit facts she has)

- **Her training:**
  - She is a Silencer, trained from age 7 in the Cradle.
  - She knows knife work, threat geometry, and limited magic (Grey debt).
  - She knows Orin is her Savant partner.

- **The mission:**
  - She is hunting “the Seed” (Renne) – Amber debt, capture or terminate.
  - She knows five teams were deployed; she knows Team 5 and Team 4 are dead.
  - She knows Grey Kestrel and the Blood‑mage were killed in the grotto (she killed the Blood‑mage).

- **Observations:**
  - She has seen Renne (the healer) and Hark (the Ghost) up close.
  - She noticed inconsistencies in Order reports (child killed by wrong weapon).
  - She saved Hark’s life with her medical kit – consciously deserting.

- **About herself:**
  - She knows she was a foundling, surrendered at birth.
  - She knows nothing about her biological parents.

### SUSPECTS

- That the Order’s narratives are false (she has seen evidence).
- That Orin knows more than he is telling her.
- That Renne might be connected to her in some way (Lina’s drawing, the grey eyes, the ache in her chest) – **but she has not consciously named this as “mother.”**

### UNAWARE (Critical blind spots)

- **About her parentage:**
  - She does NOT know that Renne is her mother.
  - She does NOT know that Hark is her father.
  - She has never heard the name “Damian Whiters.”

- **About the foundling house records:**
  - She does not know that Team 3 identified her as Renne’s daughter.
  - She does not know that Orin is keeping that secret.

- **About Hark’s identity:**
  - She knows him only as “the Ghost” – a target who killed four Order agents.
  - She does not know his real name or his connection to Renne.

- **About the future:**
  - She has no plan. She deserted. She is drifting with Renne’s group.

### MISUNDERSTANDS

- She believes the Order is fundamentally just – but she is beginning to doubt.
- She believes her own feelings are irrelevant to the mission – but she acted on them (saving Hark).

## ORIN

### KNOWS (Explicit facts he has)

- **Savant knowledge:**
  - He can read debt signatures.
  - He has access to Order ledgers, including foundling records and debt histories.

- **The truth about Alexandria:**
  - He knows Alexandria is Renne’s biological daughter (from Team 3’s report).
  - He knows the childbirth debt matches.
  - He has not told Alexandria.

- **The hunt:**
  - He knows Team 5 and Team 4 are dead (killed by Hark).
  - He knows Team 2 is dead (Kestrel by Hark, Blood‑mage by Alexandria).
  - He knows Alexandria deserted.
  - He knows Hark is comatose, not dead.
  - He knows Renne survived.

- **His own actions:**
  - He falsified the report, listing Renne as “Extinguished” and Hark as “Deceased.”
  - He has kept a private ledger with the truth.

### SUSPECTS

- That Hark and Renne were once lovers (he read the debt residue – intimate proximity in the past).
- That Alexandria might eventually discover the truth.
- That the Order will eventually audit his report.

### UNAWARE

- **About Hark’s identity:**
  - He does not know Hark’s real name (Damian Whiters) – only “the Ghost.”
  - He does not know Hark is Alexandria’s father. (The foundling records show only the mother’s debt; the father is not recorded because Renne never named him.)

- **About the backstory details:**
  - He does not know about Lea, Joel, or the FID rules (he only observed psychological repression, not the cause).

- **About Lina’s drawings:**
  - He has never seen them.

### MISUNDERSTANDS

- He believes his lie will protect them indefinitely – but it may only buy time.
- He believes he is too old to change – but his trembling handwriting suggests otherwise.

## POV RESTRICTIONS FOR WRITERS

### When writing HARK’s POV:
- **Cannot** use the words: trust, afraid, want, lonely, love (or any synonym as a felt emotion – only as external observation).
- **Cannot** reveal that Alexandria is his daughter. He has no idea.
- **Cannot** reveal that Renne’s baby survived. He believes it died.
- **Can** show his physical stress responses (tight chest, faster pulse, counting exits) as substitutes for emotion.
- **Can** show his observational hierarchy (hands, exits, weapons, faces, denied beauty).

### When writing RENNE’s POV:
- **Cannot** reveal that Alexandria is her daughter to the reader via internal monologue until she herself consciously recognizes her. (She only suspects vaguely from Lina’s drawing – but does not *know* until a later volume.)
- **Can** show her grief, her hope, her fear.
- **Cannot** know Hark’s full past (Lea, Joel) unless he told her (he didn’t).

### When writing ALEXANDRIA’s POV:
- **Cannot** know Renne is her mother. She only feels an unexplained ache.
- **Cannot** know Hark is her father.
- **Can** notice physical details (grey eyes, scarred hands) without connecting them.
- **Can** be suspicious of Orin’s silences.

### When writing ORIN’s POV:
- **Cannot** know Hark is the father (only that Renne is the mother).
- **Can** know all other secrets he has access to via ledgers.
- **Can** show his internal conflict about lying.

## SCENE‑LEVEL CHECK

Before writing any scene, ask:
1. Whose POV is this?
2. What does this character **explicitly know** at this moment in the timeline?
3. What could they **observe** with their five senses?
4. What might they **infer** (and is that inference correct or incorrect)?
5. What is **impossible** for them to know? (Keep a mental list.)

If any information in the scene cannot be traced back to that character’s knowledge or observation, cut or reassign the POV.
